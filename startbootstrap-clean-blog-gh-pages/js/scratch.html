Parker Dean 2023

The greatest story in Star Wars history!

Reasons why it has the best story:

  1. This game was pushed out in 16 months.
    • For a game to be pushed out in less than year and a half and still have as good of a story as it does is impressive.
    • The writers knew what they were doing from the moment the game starts, nothing is in the story without reason.
  2. Its a choose your own adventure.
    • There are far more options than just a linear storyline.
    • You have the ability to choose light side, dark side or straddle the middle of the two
  3. The Star Wars franchise has pulled elements from this game to use in the movies constantly
    • It is theorized that Ryan Johnson took heavy inspiration from this story when writing the 8th movie.
    • This game defined how the force is understood in Star Wars. Before it was always unknowable, but this made the force understandable
